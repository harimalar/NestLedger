# Privacy Policy

**NestLedger**
Last updated: March 8, 2025

---

## Overview

NestLedger ("the App") is built with a privacy-first philosophy. We collect **no personal data**, **no analytics**, and use **no third-party trackers or advertising SDKs**.

---

## Data Storage

All data you enter into NestLedger — including transactions, budgets, savings goals, and settings — is stored **exclusively on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.

- ✅ No data is transmitted to our servers
- ✅ No cloud sync unless you explicitly enable iCloud (handled entirely by Apple)
- ✅ No account or sign-in required

---

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync within the app, your data will be synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure in accordance with [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to this data.

---

## In-App Purchases

NestLedger offers a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase ("NestLedger Pro") processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

---

## Analytics & Tracking

NestLedger contains:
- ❌ No third-party analytics SDKs
- ❌ No advertising networks
- ❌ No tracking pixels or fingerprinting
- ❌ No crash reporting services

---

## Children's Privacy

NestLedger does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
