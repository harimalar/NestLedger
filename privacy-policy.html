Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

NestLedger is built with a privacy-first philosophy. We collect no personal data, use no analytics, and include no third-party trackers or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

All data you enter — including transactions, budgets, savings goals, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • ✅ No data is transmitted to our servers
  • ✅ No cloud sync unless you explicitly enable iCloud (handled entirely by Apple)
  • ✅ No account or sign-in required

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync within the app, your data is synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data.

In-App Purchases

NestLedger offers a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase ("NestLedger Pro"), processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

NestLedger contains:

  • ❌ No third-party analytics SDKs
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No tracking pixels or fingerprinting
  • ❌ No crash reporting services

Children's Privacy

NestLedger does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
